About

Hi, I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science in Turing Class, Zhejiang University, jointly cultivated by CKC Honors College and College of Computer Science and Technology. During my undergraduate, I am grateful to be advised by Prof. Chunhua Shen on generative visual models, Prof. Jake Zhao on data-centric AI and Prof. Yingcai Wu on visual analytics. Currently I am also a visiting research student at Stanford University, supervised by Prof. Jiajun Wu.

I am interested in computer vision and machine learning. My research experience spans topics including 3D scene understanding, generative models and video understanding. I have also done engaging projects on visual analytics and HCI.
